I am a third year PhD student at [EDF R&D](https://www.edf.fr/groupe-edf/inventer-l-avenir-de-l-energie/r-d-un-savoir-faire-mondial) and [Sorbonne University](https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/) (in the [MLIA](https://mlia.lip6.fr/) team).

I work on representation learning, transfer learning and pre-trained neural networks for time series, with an emphasis on energy applications.

I am supervised by Vincent Guigue and Nicolas Baskotis (Sorbonne University) and Ghislain Agoua (EDF R&D).
